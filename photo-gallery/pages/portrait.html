PORTRAIT PHOTOGRAPHY

About Portrait Photography

Portrait photography captures the essence of [describe the genre]. This style emphasizes [key characteristics] and requires a keen eye for [specific skills]. Film photography adds a unique dimension to Portrait work, with each film stock bringing its own character to the images.

The grain structure, color rendition, and dynamic range of film create a distinctive look that digital photography often struggles to replicate. Many photographers prefer film for Portrait photography because of its organic feel and the way it handles highlights and shadows.

When shooting Portrait on film, considerations like film speed, lighting conditions, and development techniques play crucial roles in the final outcome. Different film stocks can dramatically alter the mood and aesthetic of the photographs.
